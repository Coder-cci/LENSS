* FancyMines v1.00 *
Author: Mateusz Viste

FancyMines is a minesweeper-like game with some
graphical themes. It runs in DOS, Windows and
Linux. FancyMines is released under the terms
of the GNU/GPL license.

To run the game in windowed or fullscreen mode,
one can use following parameters:

--setfullscreen (game starts in fullscreen mode)
--setwindowed   (game starts in windowed mode)
